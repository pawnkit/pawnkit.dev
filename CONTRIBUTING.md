# Contributing

PawnKit is maintained by volunteers, so reviews may take a little time.

Guide fixes and tested examples are welcome. Write for someone trying to finish
a task, not someone reading the whole site in order.

Edit guides in `content`. Change generated facts in their owning repository.

When updating a generated source:

1. Change its tag or commit in `sources.json`.
2. Check out that revision in the matching sibling repository.
3. Run `task check`.

Keep headings in sentence case and comments short. Examples should use released
commands unless they are clearly marked as development builds.
